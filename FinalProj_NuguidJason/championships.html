Championships

The Golden State Warriors have won 7 NBA Championships across their rich history, showcasing excellence across different eras of basketball.

1947

Philadelphia Warriors won the inaugural BAA championship.

1956

Warriors led by Paul Arizin won their second title.

1975

Rick Barry led the underdog Warriors to a sweep over the Bullets.

2015

Stephen Curry and company ended a 40-year title drought.

2017

Kevin Durant joined forces with Curry and Thompson for a dominant run.

2018

Back-to-back champions cementing a modern dynasty.

2022

Curry led the Warriors to another ring, earning Finals MVP.
